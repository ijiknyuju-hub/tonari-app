# ADR-006: Revenue from growth experience, not information restriction

Status: Accepted
Date: 2026-06-15

## Context
Traditional cooking app monetization restricts recipe access behind paywalls. ChatGPT provides free recipe suggestions as a substitute, making information-based paywalls fragile. Cookpad's premium conversion declined; 味の素's AI meal planner shut down in 2026-04.

## Decision
Monetize accumulated personal growth experience: map/encyclopedia area expansion, growth archive, user-curated recipe collection, challenge suggestions. Never restrict recipe details behind a paywall.

## Rationale
- ChatGPT replaces recipe information in 3 seconds — can't compete on information
- Personal growth data (12+ made-records, island map progress) creates emotional lock-in
- "I don't want to lose my progress" is stronger than "I want to read this recipe"
- Subscription design deferred until weekly re-visit rate ≥2 is confirmed

## Alternatives Considered
- Premium recipes: rejected — instantly commoditized by AI
- Ad-only model: kept as Phase 2 bridge, not primary strategy
- B2B data sales: possible at MAU 10K+, not near-term

## Consequences
- Good: Defensible moat grows with user investment
- Risk: Requires meaningful engagement before any revenue (longer runway needed)
